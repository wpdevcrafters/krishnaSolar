Our Journey

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the rooftop solar industry, ShriKrishna Solar began as a small team of passionate engineers. Today, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted end-to-end EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) companies.

We recognized that customers were often confused by complex solar jargon and unreliable vendors. We stepped in to bridge that gap by providing complete transparency, premium Tier-1 materials, and hassle-free subsidy assistance.
