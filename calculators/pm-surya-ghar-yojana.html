📝 How to Apply — Step by Step

1

Register on pmsuryaghar.gov.in

Enter your state, DISCOM name and electricity consumer number. Verify with your registered mobile OTP. Takes 2 minutes.

2

Apply for Rooftop Solar

Fill in your roof area, preferred system capacity and upload a photo of your electricity bill. DISCOM will verify feasibility within 7–15 days.

3

Choose Empanelled Vendor

Select from the list of MNRE-empanelled vendors in your area. Compare their quotes on the portal. Do NOT pay cash — use digital payment only.

4

Installation & Net Meter

Vendor installs the system (typically 1–3 days). DISCOM inspects and installs a bidirectional net meter. This step takes 15–30 days.

5

Subsidy Credited to Bank

Upload commissioning certificate on the portal. Central subsidy of ₹30,000–₹78,000 is credited directly to your Aadhaar-linked bank account within 30 days via DBT.
