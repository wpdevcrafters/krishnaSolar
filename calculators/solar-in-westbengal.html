Solar Energy in Visakhapatnam (Vizag): Complete 2026 Guide

Vizag is AP's largest city, a naval base, and India's first smart city. APEPDCL has set a target to solar-power 50,000 households in Vizag by 2027 under the PM Surya Ghar programme. With electricity tariffs charged by APEPDCL at ₹6.80/unit and rising every year, rooftop solar has become one of the smartest long-term investments available to Visakhapatnam (Vizag) homeowners in 2026.

How Much Solar Power Can You Generate in Visakhapatnam (Vizag)?

Visakhapatnam (Vizag) receives approximately 5.3 peak sun hours per day (based on MNRE solar radiation data for this region). This means every 1 kW of rooftop solar installed here generates about 1,606 units of electricity per year.

For a typical 3 kW system — the most popular size for a 2–3 BHK home — you can expect:

  • ~13.2 units per day on average (higher in summer, lower in monsoon)
  • 4,818 units per year
  • ₹2,693/month savings on your APEPDCL electricity bill
  • Payback period: ~3.2 years after PM Surya Ghar subsidy

PM Surya Ghar Yojana Benefits for Visakhapatnam (Vizag) Households

Residents of Visakhapatnam (Vizag) in Andhra Pradesh are fully eligible for the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana. The central government provides a Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) subsidy directly to your bank account:

  • Up to 2 kW system: ₹30,000/kW (total ₹60,000 for 2 kW)
  • 2 kW to 3 kW: ₹18,000/kW for the additional capacity
  • Above 3 kW: No additional central subsidy — but net metering earnings continue
  • Maximum central subsidy: ₹78,000 for a 3 kW system

State subsidy note: No AP state top-up. Central PM Surya Ghar applies. APEPDCL has a dedicated rooftop solar helpline: 1912 for net metering queries.

Working with APEPDCL for Net Metering

In Visakhapatnam (Vizag), electricity distribution and net metering for rooftop solar is managed by APEPDCL (Eastern Power Distribution Company of AP). Here's what you need to know about solar net metering under APEPDCL:

  • Net metering limit: up to 1 MW per consumer connection
  • Meter type: Bi-directional smart meter (provided and installed by APEPDCL)
  • Billing cycle: Monthly — excess units exported to grid are credited against your consumption
  • Annual settlement: Any remaining credit units are carried forward; some DISCOMs pay a feed-in tariff for net annual export
  • Application portal: apepdcl.in

Step-by-Step: How to Get Solar in Visakhapatnam (Vizag)

  1. Check your eligibility: Use our Subsidy Calculator to see the exact PM Surya Ghar amount for your home's load and Andhra Pradesh location.
  2. Size your system: Use the Panel Requirement Calculator — enter your monthly bill in units. Most Visakhapatnam (Vizag) households need a 2–4 kW system.
  3. Register on the National Portal: Go to pmsuryaghar.gov.in, register with your Aadhaar and APEPDCL consumer number.
  4. Choose an MNRE-empanelled installer from the portal's vendor list — do not use non-empanelled vendors as your subsidy will be blocked.
  5. Get installation done: Typical completion time in Visakhapatnam (Vizag): 2–4 days for a residential system.
  6. Submit inspection documents to APEPDCL for net meter installation (usually within 15–30 days).
  7. Receive subsidy: DBT credit directly to your bank account within 30 days of net meter commissioning.
