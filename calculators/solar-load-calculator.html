Why Calculate Solar Load?

Choosing the right solar system size depends entirely on your energy consumption. If you install a system that is too small, you'll still pay a high electricity bill. If it's too big, you're wasting money on capacity you don't need.

This calculator helps you estimate your Total Connected Load (Watts) and Energy Consumption (kWh) based on the appliances you use every day.
